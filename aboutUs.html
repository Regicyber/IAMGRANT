Who We Are

iAMGRANT Pictures is a media consultancy firm that uses information and communication
technology (ICT) tools to develop robust solutions that meet the needs of any size or model
of business and organization that want to be above their competition.
Since the establishment of the company, we have provided innovative, quality media services to
the delight of our clients in the public and private sector through state of the art technology
services. The ownership group are working members of our teams involved in all aspects
of every project, from conception to preproduction to production, continuing through to the end
of the post-production and even distribution.
